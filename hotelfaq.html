Frequently Asked Questions

What time is check-in and check-out?

Check-in starts at 2:00 PM, and check-out is until 12:00 PM. Early or late times are subject to availability.

Is breakfast included in the room rate?

Yes, a complimentary breakfast is provided with every room booking.

Do you offer free Wi-Fi?

Yes, we provide free high-speed Wi-Fi across all rooms and public areas.

Do you have parking facilities?

We offer free on-site parking, including valet service upon request.

Are pets allowed?

Sorry, pets are not allowed on the hotel premises.

What is your cancellation policy?

Cancel up to 24 hours before check-in for a full refund. Late cancellations may be charged for one night.

Do you offer airport pickup/drop services?

Yes, airport transfers are available for an additional fee. Contact the front desk to arrange.

Is smoking allowed in the rooms?

No, all rooms are non-smoking. However, there are designated smoking areas outside.

Do you provide extra beds or baby cots?

Yes, both extra beds and baby cots can be arranged. Additional charges may apply.

Can I modify or extend my stay after booking?

Yes, based on room availability. Please contact the front desk for changes.

Do you have a restaurant or room service?

Yes, we have an in-house multi-cuisine restaurant and offer 24/7 room service.
